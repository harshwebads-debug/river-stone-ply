Block Boards

River Stone Block Boards are engineered to deliver unmatched strength, stability, and dimensional accuracy. Crafted using high-quality wooden battens and bonded with superior resins, they prevent bending and warping even in high humidity. Ideal for doors, tables, paneling, and partitions, our block boards ensure durability, a smooth surface finish, and long-term performance. Perfect for both home and commercial interiors that demand a combination of sturdiness and elegance.

Thickness: 19mm, 25mm
Sizes: 8' × 4', 7' × 4', 6' × 3' (custom sizes available)
  • Dimensionally stable & warp resistant
  • Anti-termite & borer treated
  • Available in MR & BWP grade
  • Made with selected hardwood battens
  • High screw and nail holding capacity
