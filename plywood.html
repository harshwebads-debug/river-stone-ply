River Stone Ply — Premium Collection

Plywood

River Stone Plywood blends strength, style, and sustainability in every sheet. Made from premium hardwood veneers and bonded with high-grade resins, it ensures long-lasting performance for both interiors and exteriors. With a smooth finish, strong screw-holding capacity, and reliable moisture resistance, it’s the ideal choice for furniture, paneling, and interior architecture — combining beauty with durability.

Thickness: 4mm, 6mm, 9mm, 12mm, 16mm, 18mm, 25mm
Sizes: 8' × 4', 7' × 4', 6' × 3' (custom sizes available)
  • Boiling Water Proof (BWP) Grade Available
  • Anti-bacterial & Anti-termite protection
  • Made from 100% hardwood timber
  • Exceptional moisture resistance
  • Superior screw & nail holding strength
