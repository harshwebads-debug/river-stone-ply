Alternate 303 Grade Plywood

River Stone Alternate 303 Grade Plywood is engineered for versatility and reliability. Made from selected hardwood veneers and bonded with high-quality Melamine Urea Formaldehyde (MUF) resin, it provides superior performance for indoor applications. Designed to deliver durability, smooth finish, and enhanced screw-holding strength, it’s an ideal choice for furniture, cabinets, paneling, and interiors. Crafted with precision and care, this plywood balances quality and affordability — perfect for everyday projects that demand lasting value.

Grade: IS:303 (MR Grade)
Thickness: 4mm to 25mm
  • Moisture Resistant (MR Grade)
  • High-quality alternate hardwood core
  • Superior nail & screw-holding strength
  • Treated for termite & borer resistance
  • Ideal for furniture & interior paneling
